View Trade Details

Applicants and Beneficiaries can see further details about the trade received from VAKT.

The following details are shown on this screen:

Trade Data information;

  1. Trade Data
  2. Goods to be Financed
  3. Contract Information
  4. Delivery Terms
  5. Documentary Requirements

Commercial Documents;

  1. Documents Attached

Cargo movement data;

  1. Cargo Movements

The Commercial Contract can be sent by VAKT (optional) and will be attached to the Trade, located under the 'Documents Attached' section.
